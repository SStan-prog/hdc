# 1st Study Session: Leveraging Generative AI

## Event Overview

This was the first study session organized by HUGA DIGITAL COLLECTIVE. We explored practical applications of generative AI in business and shared experiences among participants.

## Session Content

### Session 1: AI Fundamentals
- Characteristics and use cases of ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini
- Basics of prompt engineering
- Real-world application examples

### Session 2: Practical Business Applications
- Design applications (Misaki Tanaka)
- Programming applications (Kenta Sato)
- Marketing applications (Daisuke Suzuki)

### Workshop
Participants created actual prompts and experienced AI-assisted work.

## Participant Feedback

> "Hearing everyone's application methods gave me many ideas I want to try immediately."
